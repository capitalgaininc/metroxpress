Reliable Logistics and Transport Solutions That Save You Time!

Metro Express Services is a subsidiary of the Metro Group of Companies, a global logistics provider specializing in customs clearance and transportation for all cargo types. With decades of experience, we continuously innovate to strengthen supply chains, streamline logistics, enhance tracking capabilities, and leverage data to improve our customers' experience.

We take pride in offering world-class transport and delivery services across the globe. Our skilled team combines the latest technologies and advanced tracking systems with extensive industry experience, ensuring seamless, integrated supply chain solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients.

We identify top suppliers, certify factories, manage production, and conduct thorough final inspections. Additionally, we handle all logistics and shipping, ensuring your products reach their destination seamlessly. With Metro 247 Express, you can trust us to deliver consistent quality, every time.

At Metro Express, we leverage economies of scale to secure better prices and superior quality by importing directly from the source. We collaborate closely with suppliers to create top-tier products for our brands, always prioritizing customer needs. Our rigorous quality control process includes continuous product testing and strict adherence to high standards, along with a commitment from our suppliers to 100% social compliance. By optimizing procurement, coordinating seamlessly with customers, and ensuring cost transparency, we enhance overall import efficiency and deliver maximum value to our clients.
