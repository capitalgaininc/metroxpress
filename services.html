Overview

Our team is dedicated to tailoring the booking process to meet your specific needs. We focus on finding the best cargo solution for your shipment, considering factors such as availability, delivery deadlines, and potential opportunities to save on both time and cost. By understanding your requirements, we determine the most efficient approach, whether it’s direct air freight or exploring our sea-air service as a more suitable option.

We have over 20 years of experience in air freight and related services, we have become specialists in the field. Our close partnerships with major airlines worldwide, coupled with ongoing negotiations, ensure we always have access to the cargo space you need and can provide competitive rates, even during peak seasons.

Where possible, we also offer the assembly and disassembly of Unit Load Devices (ULDs), minimizing the risk of damage to your shipment while saving both time and costs. This is made possible by our freight stations, many of which are equipped with their own ground transportation around the airport, allowing for seamless handling and quicker turnaround times.

Stats & Charts

Our combination of company-owned and contractor assets gives us the flexibility to maintain optimal control while expanding our coverage to over 96% of towns across Australia. With 40 years of experience in Less-than-Truckload (LTL) freight, we have earned the trust of shippers of all sizes and across various commodity types.

Our LTL services span all states and territories, offering multiple weekly services to destinations that many competitors only serve sporadically, such as Darwin, Alice Springs, Newman, Mt. Isa, Launceston, and Burnie.

We take pride in delivering the best transport and shipping services available in the world. Our skilled team leverages the latest technologies, tracking, and processing software, combined with decades of expertise, to ensure your freight is shipped efficiently and reliably.
